Project 2

Type:In-semester Group Project

July 2022

  • Visual design solutions for the greater good
  • Choosing Sustainable development goals and forming apt problem statements
  • App design and ux laws application

This is Carbon, an app we made for our VDC endsem project. We were tasked to pick a sustainable development goal and come up with apt problem statements as well as doing primary and secondary research.

We decided to tackle the problem of excessive motorists and their impact on cities and their livability. We choose an app as a way to incentivise people to take more sustainable ways of transport.

Project 3

Type:Inclusive design Group Project

November 2022

  • Conducted field research and gathered insights/li>
  • Thorough understanding of design processes
  • Applied design techniques to gain more user understanding

This was a semester long project in the subject Design Processes and Perspectives(DPP) we chose the domain of inclusive design. Our final aim was to streamline communication between the deaf and abeled persons.

We conducted field research at Indian Sign Language Research and Training Center(ISLRTC) where we gathered insights, conducted interviews and proceeded to narrow down the scope of our problem.

With it being a design processes course we had the guidance of Professor Amit Sabherwal who guided us and advised us on the effectiveness of the solutions we came up with

Finally our solution came down to 3 parts; firstlr, a reactive jewellery, which vibrated and provided you feedback on what was going on your environment, this prototype was implemented using an arduino. Secondly, we had an app which translated in real time what people around you are saying and provided you the option to react and speak back. Finally, our future solution consisted of smart wearables like sunglasses which would provide real time subtitles and showed alerts.
